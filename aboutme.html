Hello and welcome to my website! I am a professor of history at XYZ University, where I teach courses on modern European history, the history of the British Empire, and the history of gender and sexuality. I received my PhD in history from ABC University and have been teaching at XYZ since 2010. My research interests include the social and cultural history of modern Europe, the history of colonialism and imperialism, and feminist and queer theory.

Outside of teaching and research, I enjoy hiking, cooking, and traveling. I am also an avid fan of the theatre and frequently attend performances in the city. You can follow me on Twitter and Instagram to stay up-to-date on my latest activities and thoughts on history and current events.
