About Ms. Linda Otoo

Ms. Linda Otoo is a passionate and experienced educator who has been teaching for over 10 years. She holds a PhD in COmputer Science from the University of Cape Coast, where she conducted research on student engagement and motivation in the classroom.

Ms. Otoo's teaching philosophy emphasizes active learning, critical thinking, and collaboration. She believes that students learn best when they are actively engaged in the learning process and when they are given opportunities to apply what they have learned in real-world contexts.

Ms. Otoo has received numerous awards for her teaching, including the Outstanding Teacher Award from the National Association for Gifted Children and the Distinguished Teaching Award from the University of Cape Coast.
